Interactive Chatbot



This project aims to develop an intelligent chatbot with three key features: Small Talk, Movie Information Querying, and Assistance with Ticket Booking. Our chatbot utilizes natural language processing techniques to provide engaging and interactive conversations.


Small Talk

The chatbot incorporates a comprehensive set of questions and answers for small talk, allowing users to have casual and friendly conversations. It provides responses to various queries and engages users in a lively manner, making the interaction enjoyable and natural.


Movie Information Querying

Users can ask the chatbot about specific movies, including plot summaries, cast members, directors, genres, and more. The chatbot leverages a rich dataset to deliver accurate and informative responses, enriching the user's movie knowledge.


Assistance with Ticket Booking

In addition to providing movie information, our chatbot offers assistance with ticket booking for Savoy Cinema Nottingham. Users can inquire about showtimes and ticket availability, and even book tickets through the chatbot. The chatbot seamlessly enables users to have a hassle-free ticket booking experience.
